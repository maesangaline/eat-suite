Butternut Squash Soup

It's soup! Paleo + Keto friendly

Tip: Sprinkle pumpkin seeds on top!

Yields Prep Time Cook Time
10 Servings 00 Hours 30 Minutes 00 Hours 40 Minutes

Ingredients

One 2-3lbs   butternut squash peeled, seeded, and cubed

2 tbsp   unsalted butter

1 medium sized   onion, chopped

6 cups   chicken stock

Pinch of   Nutmeg, to taste

Pinch of   Salt & Pepper, to taste

Pinch of   Cayenne pepper, to taste

DIRECTIONS

01   Cut squash into 1 inch chunks

02   Melt butter in a pot. Add onion and cook until translucent (approximately 8 minutes

03   Add squash and chicken stock

04   Bring to a simmer and cook until squash is tender (about 15 to 20 minutes)

05   Remove squash chunks and place in a blender to puree

06   Return blended squash to pot. Stir and season with nutmeg, salt, pepper, and cayenne
