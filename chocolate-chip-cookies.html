Chocolate Chip Cookies

An ooey gooey family favorite 

These are the chocolate chip cookies I bake for every holiday. Timing is everything with these!

Tip: Cookies should appear slightly underbaked after their bake time is up. This is normal!

Yields Prep Time Cook Time
14 Servings 02  Hours 15 Minutes 00 Hours 11 Minutes

Ingredients

¾ cup   unsalted butter

¾ cup   light brown sugar

¼ cup   granulated sugar

1 large   egg

1 tsp   vanilla extract

2 cups   flour

3.5 ounce packet   instant vanilla pudding

1 tsp   baking soda

Pinch of salt   to taste

2 cups   semi-sweet chocolate chips

DIRECTIONS

01  Preheat oven to 350°F

02  Combine butter, sugars, egg, & vanilla - beat on medium-high speed until creamed

03   Scrape down the sides of the bowl, add flour, pudding mix, baking soda, and salt. Mix on low speed until thoroughly combined.

04   Add in chocolate chips

05   Form mounds of dough. Roll them into 1in balls and flatten slightly

06   Place mounds on a large plate or tray, cover with plastic-wrap. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Do not bake unchilled dough! Cookies will be thing, flat, and will not spread correctly if not chilled!

07   Bake for 11 minutes
