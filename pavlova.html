Pavlova

Delicious. Simple. Adaptable.  

You can substitute any type of sugar alternative, such as Swerve, so long as it is confectioners (powdered)!

Yields Prep Time Cook Time
06 Servings 00 Hours 15 Minutes 01 Hours 38 Minutes

Ingredients

4   egg whites carefully separated

6 tbsp   confectioners sugar

½ tsp   vanilla extract

¼ tsp   cream of tartar

DIRECTIONS

01   In a glass bowl, add egg whites, sweetener, vanilla, and crem of tartar

02   Use a hand mixer to beat egg whites until they are glossy and form small peaks. Do not whip until stiff!

03   Line a baking sheet with parchment paper

04   Make 5 circles of the mixture

05   Bake for 18 minutes

06   Turn oven temperature down to 200 degrees and bake for an additional 20 minutes

07   Turn oven of and let cool with door closed (approximately 1 hour)
